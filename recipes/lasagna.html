Lasagna

A comforting lasagna made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese creates the ultimate Italian dish that’s perfect for any gathering.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Béchamel sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian herbs (oregano, basil)

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook Meat: Brown the meat in a pan and add tomato sauce; simmer.
  3. Layer Ingredients: In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  4. Repeat: Continue layering until all ingredients are used, ending with mozzarella.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake for an additional 15 minutes.
  6. Cool: Let it sit for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
