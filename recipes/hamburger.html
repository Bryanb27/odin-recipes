Hamburger

A juicy hamburger made with a grilled beef patty, fresh lettuce, ripe tomato, and special sauce, all nestled between soft buns, makes for the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Pickles
  • Onions (optional)
  • Mustard and ketchup
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Form Patties: Shape ground beef into patties, season with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill: Cook patties on a grill or pan for about 4-5 minutes per side.
  3. Toast Buns: Lightly toast the burger buns on the grill.
  4. Assemble: Place the cooked patty on the bottom bun, add cheese if desired.
  5. Add Toppings: Layer lettuce, tomato, pickles, and onions on top.
  6. Top and Serve: Add the top bun, secure with a skewer if needed, and serve with condiments.
