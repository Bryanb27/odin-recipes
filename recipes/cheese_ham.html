Cheese with Ham

A cheese and ham sandwich is a classic comfort food, combining the savory flavors of ham with the creamy, melty goodness of cheese. This simple yet satisfying dish can be enjoyed hot or cold and is perfect for a quick lunch or snack.

Ingredients

  • Bread: Your choice of 2 slices (white, whole grain, sourdough, etc.)
  • Cheese: 2 slices (cheddar, Swiss, provolone, or your favorite)
  • Ham: 2-3 slices (deli ham, smoked ham, or honey ham)
  • Spread: Butter or mayonnaise (optional, for added flavor)
  • Extras: Lettuce, tomato, or pickles (optional, for crunch)

Steps

  1. Prep the Bread: If using, spread butter or mayonnaise on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Layer Ingredients: On the unbuttered side of one slice, place a slice of cheese. Add the ham, then top with the second slice of cheese.
  3. Assemble: Place the second slice of bread on top, buttered side facing out.
  4. Cook (Optional): Heat a skillet over medium heat. Grill the sandwich for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  5. Serve: Remove from heat, cut if desired, and enjoy your delicious cheese and ham sandwich!
