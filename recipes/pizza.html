Pizza

A classic Margherita pizza features a thin, crispy crust topped with tangy tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, aromatic basil leaves, and a drizzle of olive oil. The result is a harmonious blend of flavors that celebrates simplicity.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce (canned or homemade)
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Optional: Parmesan cheese, crushed red pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Heat your oven to the highest setting (usually around 475°F to 500°F).
  2. Prepare Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Add Sauce: Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border.
  4. Add Cheese: Tear fresh mozzarella into pieces and scatter them over the sauce.
  5. Bake: Transfer the pizza to a preheated pizza stone or baking sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Garnish: Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.
  7. Slice and Serve: Let it cool slightly, slice, and enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
